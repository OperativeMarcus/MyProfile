Interests

What is my interest in IT

I have been interested in information technology for as long as I can remember.
As a young child I remember my dad getting a new Windows 95 computer for work.
I would just sit there and watch him working, thinking to myself how cool it was that he didn’t have to write things down on paper.
As I grew older and started playing video games and using technology more frequently, I began to appreciate how incredible the world of IT really is, and how important it is to us as a society.
During high school I began to research and teach myself as much as I could, and I have fallen in love with IT and learning. There is something so liberating about being able to use technology efficiently to help achieve a goal.

Why did I choose to come to RMIT?

I have been working in various IT roles for the last 7 years, during this time I have always considered further study and have spoken about it to various friends, co-workers and people in the industry. RMIT has been recommended to me consistently by these people, and when the time came to continue my studies, I knew that RMIT would be the best option for me.

What do I expect to learn during my studies?

As much as possible.

I would really like to learn more about web design and coding, as I find coding to be a brilliant mix of creativity and logical problem solving. I already have some experience with HTML/CSS/JS as I recently began to do some web dev work on the side, so I have been spending a lot of time on stackexchange and github trying to get better at those three languages. Outside of those specific skills I just want to use all the resources and time I have available to absorb as much information as possible. I am also excited to be surrounded by other people who are passionate about learning, and hope to learn from other people’s own experiences.
